We Are Little Ghost


Fostering creative conversations at our pop-up café, Little Ghost creates a pleasant atmospheres in unique locations. With a different story for each pop-up, embrace your inner ghoul and meet like-minded individuals.

Crayons


Our crayons tell a story. Through the concept of scent and colour our crayons will take you through a journey with your art. Fun for all ages and available in a variety of colours and scents stop by the cafe or find us at our next pop up!




Find Us


Located in the scenic River Valley, Little Ghost is just a short walk away from transit and Downtown Edmonton.
